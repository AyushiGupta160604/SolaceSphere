Understanding Mood Tracker

The first of it's kind SolaceSphere Mood Tracker

Embark on a journey of self-discovery and emotional well-being with our innovative Mood Tracker. Designed to be your personal companion on the path to better mental health, the SolaceSphere Mood Tracker provides a visually appealing and user-friendly platform for monitoring and understanding your daily moods.

Key Features:

Intuitive Design: Immerse yourself in a soothing ambiance with our carefully curated background images that set the tone for your mood-tracking experience.
Emoji-Based Mood Selection: Express your emotions effortlessly by choosing from a selection of emojis that best represent your feelings, including options like Happy, Sad, Angry, Disappointed, and Satisfied.
Dynamic Chart Visualization: Witness your mood patterns unfold dynamically through an interactive line chart. Visualize the intensity of your emotions on a scale of 1 to 10, creating a comprehensive overview of your mood fluctuations over time.
User Interaction: The Mood Tracker encourages active engagement by allowing you to add, save, and remove mood entries. Take charge of your emotional well-being with a few clicks.
Persistent Data Storage: Your mood data is securely stored using local storage, ensuring that your progress is preserved across sessions. Easily pick up where you left off and track your emotional journey seamlessly.
Average Mood Calculation: Receive insightful feedback on your overall mood by calculating the average intensity. The app dynamically adjusts the display to indicate whether your mood trends towards positivity, neutrality, or negativity.

How to use:

Add Mood: Choose an emoji that mirrors your current emotion and rate its intensity on a scale of 1 to 10.
Save Mood: Safeguard your mood data for future reflections and analysis. Your entries will be preserved for the next time you visit.
Remove Mood: Feel the freedom to edit your mood history by removing entries as needed.

Whether you are seeking self-awareness or aiming for a happier, more balanced life, the SolaceSphere Mood Tracker is here to support you. Start your journey towards emotional well-being today.

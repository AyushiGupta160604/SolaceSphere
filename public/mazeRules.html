Maze Goal

Introduction:

Welcome to Maze Goal, the ultimate maze game that will keep you entertained for hours! Immerse yourself in a world of challenging puzzles, and endless fun. Are you ready to embark on this challenging adventure?

How to Play:

Objective: Your goal is to move the basketball towards the net by clearing confusing maze pathway. The lesser moves you take the better your analysis of moves is!
Scoring Baskets: Analyze each step clearly and move the basketball towards the net in least possible moves. To moves, use arrow keys use the mouse. You can choose the level of game from easy to extreme.
Score: Keep track of your performance with the help of your number of moves that will be displayed after each game.
